How It Works: Step by Step

Step 1ï¸âƒ£: Worker Registration

  1. Worker clicks "Create Profile" button
  2. Fills personal details: name, mobile, age, gender

  4. Enters domicile number for identity verification

  3. Enters contact information: name, mobile
  4. Specifies rental terms: monthly rent, agreement duration
  5. Indicates worker capacity of property
  6. System generates unique Landlord ID
  7. Gets verification access immediately
  8. Can now verify tenant credentials

Step 3ï¸âƒ£: Verification & Linking

  1. Worker approaches landlord for rental
  2. Landlord opens "Verify Credentials" feature
  3. Enters worker name, domicile number, state
  4. System checks database in real-time
  5. If verified: Shows worker profile, guardian info, work history
  6. If failed: Sends alert to police dashboard with beep sound
  7. On successful verification, landlord links worker to property
  8. Worker-Landlord connection recorded with move-in date
  9. Data visible in central dashboard for authorities

Frequently Asked Questions

â“ Is SetuID free to use?

Answer: Yes, SetuID is completely free for migrant workers. It's a public service initiative to help workers maintain portable digital identity. Landlords and employers can verify workers at no cost. Government authorities get full dashboard access.

â“ Is my data safe and private?

Answer: Absolutely. SetuID is built on consent-based data sharing. Your information is only visible when you explicitly allow verification. All data is encrypted and stored securely. You control who sees what. We never sell or share your data with third parties. Password-protected access ensures only authorized personnel can view databases.

â“ Do I need internet to use SetuID?

Answer: Currently, verification requires internet connection for real-time database checking. However, in future versions, we're developing QR code-based offline verification that works without internet. Workers will be able to generate QR codes containing verified information that can be scanned offline.

â“ What if I lose my phone or documents?

Answer: Your SetuID profile is stored in the cloud, not just on your device. You can access it from any device by logging in with your credentials. Even if you lose physical documents, your digital profile remains safe and accessible. Guardian contact information is always available for emergencies.

â“ Can I update my information later?

Answer: Yes, you can update your profile anytime - change mobile number, update current address, add new work experience, modify guardian details, or upload new documents. Changes are reflected immediately across the system. Verification history is maintained for audit purposes.

â“ What if verification fails?

Answer: If verification fails, it means the entered details don't match any registered worker in the database. This triggers an automatic alert to the police dashboard for investigation. Failed verifications could indicate data entry errors, unregistered workers, or potentially fraudulent attempts. Workers are encouraged to register before seeking rental/employment.

â“ How does this help my family back home?

Answer: Your family is registered as emergency contacts in the system. If any incident occurs - accident, medical emergency, or legal issue - authorities can immediately contact your family using the information you provided. This ensures your family is never left wondering about your safety.

â“ Can I register multiple family members?

Answer: Yes, if you're traveling with family, you can indicate "Moving with Family" during registration and add details of all family members including their names, ages, gender, and relation to you. This helps landlords understand your situation and assists authorities in family-based emergencies.

â“ What documents do I need to register?

Answer: You need: (1) A valid Domicile Number or state identity proof, (2) Niwas Praman Patra (residence certificate) - can be uploaded as PDF/image, (3) A recent photograph, (4) Mobile number, (5) Guardian contact details. All documents are uploaded digitally during registration.

â“ Is this an official government system?

Answer: No, SetuID is currently a prototype developed for GDG Hackathon 2025 by Team "404: Error Handled". It is NOT affiliated with any government authority. However, the system is designed to complement existing government initiatives and can be integrated with official databases if adopted by authorities in the future.

â“ Which cities/states does this cover?

Answer: The current prototype is pan-India compatible. Workers from any state can register, and verification works across all states. The system is designed to handle inter-state migration seamlessly. Future versions will include city-specific features and local language support.

â“ How long does registration take?

Answer: Complete worker registration takes approximately 10-15 minutes. You'll need to fill personal details, upload documents, add guardian information, and optionally add family details. Landlord registration takes about 5 minutes. Once registered, your profile is active immediately and ready for verification.

â“ What happens to my data when I move back home?

Answer: Your SetuID profile remains active indefinitely. When you return to your native place, you can update your current location in the system. If you migrate again in the future, your profile is ready to use immediately without re-registration. Your work history and references accumulate over time, making future verifications even easier.
